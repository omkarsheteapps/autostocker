AutoStocker is a Chrome browser extension that helps users easily add stock symbols from Chartink to their trading watchlists on platforms like Angel One and Kite.

1. What Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

The extension uses Chrome's chrome.storage API to temporarily store the stock symbols you input, only within your own browser. This information is never sent to any external server or third-party service.

2. Permissions

AutoStocker uses the following permissions:

  • storage: To temporarily store stock symbols locally
  • activeTab & scripting: To insert the stock symbols into the currently active tab

These permissions are strictly used to deliver the extension's core functionality.

3. No Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. No user data is collected, tracked, or transmitted in any form.
